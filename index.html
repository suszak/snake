How to play?

Press spacebar or any arrow to begin game or pause.

Hold keyboard arrows to speed up your snake.

Use keyboard arrows:

Use "z" to speed up or "x" to speed down.

Collect fruits (red squares) to earn points (+1), but remember it increase your length.

Eat mice (orange squares) to earn bonus points (+2).

Try not to hit the wall and do not eat yourself!
