How to play?

Press spacebar or any arrow to begin game and spacebar to pause.

Hold keyboard arrows to speed up your snake.

Eat mice (orange squares) to earn bonus points (+2).

Try not to hit the wall and do not eat yourself!

If you want to change game speed restart game or just lose recent.
